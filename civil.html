Department of Civil Engineering

Designing the future with strength and sustainability.

About the Department

The Civil Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic - Junagadh is dedicated to equipping students with a strong foundation in the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure. The department offers state-of-the-art laboratories, industry-relevant curriculum, and hands-on training to ensure that students gain practical skills alongside theoretical knowledge. Our faculty members, who are experts in the field, provide personalized guidance to nurture the next generation of engineers. Students are encouraged to participate in various workshops, site visits, and project-based learning to enhance their problem-solving and critical thinking abilities. With a focus on sustainable development and modern construction technologies, our Civil Engineering program prepares students to tackle the challenges of the rapidly evolving built environment.

Our Vision

To produce globally compatible civil engineers with core values.

Our Mission

  • To impart quality technical education to contribute to the field of civil engineering.
  • Develop competency to work as an entrepreneur, leader, team member in the field of civil engineering.
  • Develop ethical values and a sense of responsibility towards society and the sustainability of the environment.

Program Educational Objectives (PEO)

  • PEO-1: Pursue scientific and technical careers beginning with entry level civil engineering positions in industry and higher education.
  • PEO-2: Learn soft skills along with technical knowledge in civil engineering and entrepreneurship.
  • PEO-3: Become responsible citizens have moral value and cope with recent development in the field of civil engineering.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  • PSO-1: Supervise civil engineering projects in the field of highway and transportation engineering.
  • PSO-2: Supervise civil engineering projects in the field of water resource engineering.
  • PSO-3: Incorporate sustainable materials, methods and advanced construction technology to enhance environmental responsibility.

ğŸ’» Facilities

Our department houses cutting-edge labs that inspire innovation, exploration, and hands-on practice.

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab in the Civil Engineering Department is equipped with industry-standard software such as AutoCAD. It provides students with hands-on experience in drafting, and 3D modeling, essential for planning and designing modern infrastructure projects.

Surveying Lab

The Surveying Lab in the Civil Engineering Department is equipped with modern instruments like total stations, theodolites, and digital levels. It enables students to gain practical experience in land measurement, contouring, and layout of construction projects, essential for fieldwork and site planning.

Concrete Lab

The Concrete Lab provides hands-on training in testing materials like cement, aggregates, and concrete mixes. Students learn to perform strength tests, workability assessments, and mix design procedures, building a strong foundation in quality control and construction practices.

Hydraulics Lab

The Hydraulics Lab is equipped to demonstrate fundamental fluid mechanics concepts through practical experiments. Students gain hands-on experience in flow measurement, pressure analysis, and performance testing of hydraulic machines, enhancing their understanding of water flow behavior in civil engineering applications.
