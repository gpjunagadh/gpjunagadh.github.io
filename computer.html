About the Department

The Department of Computer Engineering at Government Polytechnic Junagadh aims to impart quality technical education with a strong emphasis on practical and project-based learning. With an experienced faculty team and state-of-the-art lab facilities, the department nurtures innovation, encourages research, and ensures that students are ready to face the ever-evolving technological world with confidence and skills.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in technical education and innovation to meet industry demands and societal needs.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education through hands-on experience, research, and industry collaboration.

Program Educational Objectives (PEO)

  • To prepare students for successful careers in industry and academics.
  • To enhance professional competence through lifelong learning.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSO)

  • Ability to design and develop computer-based systems using modern tools.
  • Apply programming concepts for real-world problem solving.

💻 Facilities

Our department houses cutting-edge labs that inspire innovation, exploration, and hands-on practice.

Programming Lab

The Programming Lab is equipped with modern PCs and IDEs, enabling students to practice software development, data structures, and algorithms. It provides a hands-on environment to strengthen coding skills and problem-solving abilities.

Mobile App Development Lab

The Mobile App Development Lab provides hands-on training in building Android and iOS applications using tools like Java, Kotlin, or Flutter, helping students develop user-friendly mobile solutions.
