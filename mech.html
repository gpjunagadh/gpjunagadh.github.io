Department of Mechanical Engineering

Powering innovation through motion and mechanics.

About the Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic - Junagadh is committed to developing skilled engineers who are proficient in the principles of mechanics, thermodynamics, and manufacturing processes. The department boasts well-equipped workshops, labs, and 3D modelling software, enabling students to gain practical experience in real-world applications. Our experienced faculty guide students through a curriculum that integrates theoretical knowledge with hands-on projects, fostering innovation and technical expertise. The department also offers opportunities for students to engage in industry internships, workshops, and competitions to refine their skills. With a strong emphasis on solid modelling, manufacturing, and thermal systems, our Mechanical Engineering program prepares diploma engineers to contribute to the advancement of modern industries and technologies.

Our Vision

To prepare globally accepted mechanical engineers with profound technical skills, entrepreneurial ability and social responsibility.

Our Mission

  • To enable students to perform excellent technical skills and impart knowledge to meet the need of the current industrial global scenario.
  • To enhance the self-employment qualities of students.
  • To cultivate ethical values in students.

üíª Facilities

Our department houses cutting-edge labs that inspire innovation, exploration, and hands-on practice.

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab in the Mechanical Engineering Department is fully equipped with advanced software like AutoCAD and Autodesk Inventor, enabling students to design and model mechanical systems. These tools provide hands-on experience in solid modeling, 2D drafting, and 3D simulations, enhancing practical skills for future engineers.

Thermal Lab

The Thermal Lab in the Mechanical Engineering Department offers hands-on experience in heat transfer and thermodynamics. Equipped with setups like heat exchangers, refrigeration systems, and combustion units, it helps students connect theory with real-world thermal applications.

Workshop

The Mechanical Engineering Workshop offers hands-on training in core manufacturing practices like carpentry, tin smithy, spot welding, and fitting. It helps students develop basic fabrication skills, tool handling, and a solid understanding of traditional manufacturing techniques.

Machine Shop

The Machine Shop offers hands-on training in machining operations using tools like lathes, milling, drilling, and shaping machines. It helps students develop skills in precision machining and component fabrication essential to mechanical engineering.

Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulics Machine Lab

The Fluid Mechanics and Hydraulic Machines Lab provides hands-on experience with turbines, pumps, and flow devices, helping students grasp fluid behavior and energy conversion fundamentals essential to mechanical engineering.

Measurement & Instrumentation Lab

The Measurement and Instrumentation Lab offers hands-on training with instruments measuring temperature, pressure, displacement, and strain, helping students develop skills in accurate data acquisition and analysis essential for engineering.

Drawing Hall

The Drawing Hall provides space and tools for students to practice engineering drawing, developing skills in technical sketching and component visualization essential for effective design communication.

Project Lab

The Project Lab fosters innovation by providing a space for hands-on learning and teamwork, showcasing past student projects to inspire practical application and problem-solving skills in engineering.
