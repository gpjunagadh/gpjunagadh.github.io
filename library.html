Campus Library

A world of knowledge awaits in a quiet, focused space.

Facilities & Highlights:
  • 📘 5000+ Technical & Reference Books
  • 💻 Digital Access & e-Journals
  • 🪑 Separate Reading Zone
  • 🧾 Daily Newspapers & Magazines
  • 🕘 Open from 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM
Membership:

All students and faculty are registered members of the library. Books can be borrowed using their ID card from the circulation desk.

Library Rules:
  • Maintain silence and discipline.
  • No personal books allowed inside reading area.
  • Handle books carefully; damage may lead to penalty.
  • Return books within the due date to avoid fines.
💡 Note: Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited in the library premises.
