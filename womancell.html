Women Development Cell

Creating a secure and supportive environment for every woman on campus.

Vision:

To empower female students and staff, ensuring their full participation in academic, social, and cultural life at the institute.

Objectives:
  • 🌸 To promote gender equality and respect on campus
  • 🌸 To address grievances and provide guidance to female students
  • 🌸 To conduct awareness programs, workshops and self-defense sessions
  • 🌸 To support personal and professional growth of girl students
Initiatives and Support:

The cell organizes events like Women's Day celebrations, gender sensitization workshops, guest lectures by eminent women leaders, and career mentoring sessions.
