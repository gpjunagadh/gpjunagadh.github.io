About the Department

The Electrical Engineering Department at Government Polytechnic - Junagadh focuses on producing skilled professionals who can design, develop, and maintain electrical systems essential to various industries. With state-of-the-art laboratories and practical training, students gain deep knowledge in areas like power generation, electrical circuits, and renewable energy systems. The curriculum blends theoretical instruction with hands-on experiences to ensure students are industry-ready upon graduation. Our expert faculty members encourage critical thinking and problem-solving through projects and real-world applications. Emphasizing areas such as electrical system design, power distribution, and control systems, the department equips students with the skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving field of electrical engineering.

üíª Facilities

Our department houses cutting-edge labs that inspire innovation, exploration, and hands-on practice.

AC Machine Lab

The AC Machine Lab offers hands-on training with transformers, alternators, and induction motors, helping students understand the operation and characteristics of AC machines essential for electrical engineering.

DC Machine Lab

The DC Machine Lab provides practical experience with DC motors and generators, enabling students to learn their construction, operation, and performance essential for electrical engineering applications.

Electrical Power Utilization

The Electrical Power Utilization Lab offers hands-on experience with lighting, heating, and electric drives, helping students understand efficient power usage in industrial and domestic applications.

DC Circuit Lab

The DC Circuit Lab provides practical training in analyzing and verifying DC circuit concepts, helping students understand current, voltage, resistance, and network theorems through hands-on experiments.

AC Circuit Lab

The AC Circuit Lab offers hands-on experience in analyzing AC circuits, enabling students to understand impedance, power factor, resonance, and circuit theorems essential for electrical engineering.

Power Electronics Lab

The Power Electronics Lab provides practical training with devices like SCRs, MOSFETs, and inverters, helping students understand the control and conversion of electrical power in various applications.

Switchgear Protection Lab

The Switchgear Protection Lab offers hands-on experience with circuit breakers, relays, and protection systems, helping students learn fault detection and safe operation of electrical power systems.
