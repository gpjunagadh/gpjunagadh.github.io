Right to Information (RTI)

Transparency | Accountability | Public Access

📘 What is RTI?

The Right to Information Act, 2005 empowers citizens to seek information from public authorities, promoting transparency and accountability in government functioning.

📝 How to Apply
  • Submit your RTI request in writing.
  • Address it to the Public Information Officer (PIO).
  • Pay the prescribed fee.
  • Receive response within 30 days.
