Anti Ragging Policy

Creating a safe, inclusive and respectful campus environment for all.

What is Ragging?

Ragging is any act of physical or mental abuse, bullying or harassment intentionally done by students, often in the name of induction or bonding. At Government Polytechnic - Junagadh, we have a strict zero-tolerance policy against it.

Prohibited Activities Include:
  • 🔴 Physical abuse or intimidation
  • 🔴 Verbal harassment or humiliation
  • 🔴 Forcing students to perform unwelcome tasks
  • 🔴 Isolating, targeting or ridiculing any individual
⚠️ Note: Any student found involved in ragging activities will face disciplinary action including suspension, expulsion, or legal proceedings as per AICTE and UGC regulations.
Report Ragging

If you or someone you know is being ragged, please report immediately to the Anti-Ragging Committee.
